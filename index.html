Welcome!

Peer Review Research Hub is an interactive platform that explores the effectiveness of peer reviews across different types of courses and assessment formats.

Project Background & Goals

In today‚Äôs education landscape, peer review plays a crucial role in enhancing student engagement and critical thinking. However, different subjects‚Äîlike computer programming vs. essay writing‚Äîmay have drastically different peer review effectiveness. Meanwhile, AI-based grading systems are emerging, raising questions about how they compare with human assessments.

Our project sets out to:

  • Compare consistency and fairness of peer reviews in coding vs. essay tasks
  • Evaluate AI vs. human grading results
  • Gather suggestions to improve peer review processes

Explore the Comparison page for interactive charts and data analysis, or submit your own Suggestions to help us refine the peer review approach!
